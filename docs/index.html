receipt-scanner

Scan your receipts and keep a running expense log — entirely in your browser with tesseract.js. Nothing is uploaded and your log stays in your browser's storage. The original project (GitHub) is Python: OpenCV preprocessing + Tesseract + regex parsing, ported to JS here so anyone can use it.
